About Us

Write a Robot has been established as a technology-based company in India. Our traditional business model is to provide better platform to the students to Learn more about the underlying technology, Discover your own aptitudes and creative flair , create interests across many platforms, Keeping pace with new ideas and techniques, Raise the bar in robotics and related technologies ,also we visualize the ideas of our clients in our research and development efforts.

Every year, more than 1.5 Million students graduate in India. Out of which 97% of graduating students want their jobs either in software or core engineering. But only 7% engineering graduates are employable in the core industries. As technological enthusiasts, We formed a group to reduce the gap between the number of students completed graduation and no of students employable in the core industries.
